## 1. Payroll Topology

### 1.1 Compensation Philosophy

NOVA BLOCKS compensation philosophy is based on:

- Market competitiveness in the AI, blockchain, and fintech sectors
- Performance-based incentives tied to company and individual achievements
- Equity participation to align employee interests with long-term company success
- Global consistency with local market adaptations

### 1.2 Salary Structures

#### Executive Leadership

| Position | Base Salary Range (USD) | Annual Bonus Target | Equity Grant |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CEO | $750,000 - $1,200,000 | 100% of base | 2.0% company equity |
| CTO | $500,000 - $800,000 | 75% of base | 1.5% company equity |
| CFO | $450,000 - $700,000 | 75% of base | 1.2% company equity |
| COO | $400,000 - $650,000 | 70% of base | 1.0% company equity |
| CMO | $350,000 - $550,000 | 65% of base | 0.8% company equity |
| CCO/CDO | $300,000 - $500,000 | 60% of base | 0.6% company equity |

#### Technical Roles

| Position | Base Salary Range (USD) | Annual Bonus Target | Equity Grant |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sr. Eng/Arch | $180,000-$280,000 | 35-45% base | 0.15-0.25% eq. |
| Eng/Dev | $130,000-$200,000 | 25-35% base | 0.08-0.15% eq. |

| Data Sci./AI Spec. | $150,000-$250,000 | 30-40% base | 0.1-0.2% eq. |

### 1.3 Performance-Based Compensation

- **Performance Metrics**: 70% company performance, 30% individual performance
- **Company Metrics**: Revenue growth, profitability, strategic milestones
- **Individual Metrics**: KPIs, project delivery, innovation contributions
- **Payout Timing**: 60% at year-end, 40% deferred for 2 years

### 1.4 Benefits Package

#### Health & Wellness

- **Medical Insurance**: 100% premium coverage for employee, 75% for dependents
- **Dental & Vision**: Comprehensive coverage included
- **Mental Health**: Unlimited access to counseling services
- **Wellness Programs**: Gym memberships, nutrition counseling, health screenings

#### Retirement & Savings

- **401(k)/RRSP**: 6% company match (up to employee contribution limit)
- **Pension Plan**: Defined contribution with 8% company contribution
- **Stock Purchase Plan**: Tax-advantaged employee stock purchases

#### Time Off & Leave

- **Vacation**: 25 days annually (increases with tenure)
- **Sick Leave**: 15 days annually
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks for secondary
- **Sabbatical**: 3-month paid sabbatical after 5 years service

### 1.5 Global Payroll Considerations

#### Geographic Adjustments

| Region | Base Adjustment | Benefits Compliance | Tax Optimization |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| North America | +0-5% | ACA, HIPAA, ERISA | 401(k) tax advantages |
| Europe | +10-15% | GDPR, Working Time Directive | Pension tax relief |
| Asia-Pacific | -5% to +5% | Local labor laws | Tax-efficient structures |
| Australia | +15-20% | Fair Work Act | Superannuation requirements |
| Canada | +5-10% | Provincial labor codes | RRSP tax benefits |

### 1.6 Payroll Processing Procedures

- **Pay Cycles**: Bi-weekly (26 pay periods annually)
- **Pay Dates**: Every other Friday
- **Direct Deposit**: Mandatory for all employees
- **Payroll Calendar**: Published quarterly with holiday adjustments

## 3. End-to-End System Topology

### 3.1 Actual Repository Areas

| Area | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `nova_blocks/frontend/` | React frontend application |
| `nova_blocks/backend/` | Node.js/Express backend application |
| `nova_blocks/finance/` | Python-based finance and trading experiments |
| `nova_blocks/scripts/` | Benchmark and utility scripts |

### 3.2 Frontend Topology

- React single-page application
- Static assets under `nova_blocks/frontend/public/`
- Source code under `nova_blocks/frontend/src/`

### 3.3 Backend Topology

- Express-based Node.js service
- Jest-based test runner configured at the repository level
- Models, middleware, and scripts under `nova_blocks/backend/`

### 3.4 Finance and Benchmarking Topology

- Python modules for trading, backtesting, and AI experiments
- Benchmark scripts for Blackwell-related diagnostics and reporting
- Experimental and research-oriented rather than production-hardened

### 3.5 Documentation Topology

- `README.md` - Repository summary and setup notes
- `nova_blocks/*.md` - Corporate, revenue, and planning documents
- `docs-site/` - Docusaurus documentation site
