# NOVA BLOCKS Payroll Policy

## Overview

This comprehensive payroll policy outlines compensation structures, benefits, and procedures for NOVA BLOCKS Inc. and its global subsidiaries. The policy ensures competitive compensation aligned with industry standards, performance-driven rewards, and compliance with international labor regulations.

## Compensation Philosophy

NOVA BLOCKS compensation philosophy is based on:

- Market competitiveness in the AI, blockchain, and fintech sectors
- Performance-based incentives tied to company and individual achievements
- Equity participation to align employee interests with long-term company success
- Global consistency with local market adaptations

## Performance-Based Compensation

### Annual Bonus Structure

- **Performance Metrics**: 70% company performance, 30% individual performance
- **Company Metrics**: Revenue growth, profitability, strategic milestones
- **Individual Metrics**: KPIs, project delivery, innovation contributions
- **Payout Timing**: 60% at year-end, 40% deferred for 2 years

### Long-Term Incentives

- **Stock Options**: Time-vested over 4 years, expire in 10 years
- **RSUs**: Performance-vested based on company milestones
- **ESPP**: 15% discount on company stock purchases

## Benefits Package

### Health & Wellness

- **Medical Insurance**: 100% premium coverage for employee, 75% for dependents
- **Dental & Vision**: Comprehensive coverage included
- **Mental Health**: Unlimited access to counseling services
- **Wellness Programs**: Gym memberships, nutrition counseling, health screenings

### Retirement & Savings

- **401(k)/RRSP**: 6% company match (up to employee contribution limit)
- **Pension Plan**: Defined contribution with 8% company contribution
- **Stock Purchase Plan**: Tax-advantaged employee stock purchases

### Time Off & Leave

- **Vacation**: 25 days annually (increases with tenure)
- **Sick Leave**: 15 days annually
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks for secondary
- **Personal Days**: 5 days annually for personal matters
- **Sabbatical**: 3-month paid sabbatical after 5 years service

### Professional Development

- **Training Budget**: $5,000 annually per employee
- **Conference Attendance**: Full coverage for industry events
- **Education Reimbursement**: 100% for job-related degrees/certifications
- **Mentorship Program**: Executive mentoring for high-potential employees

### Additional Perks

- **Remote Work**: Flexible work arrangements
- **Equipment Allowance**: $2,000 annually for home office setup
- **Commuter Benefits**: Transit passes or parking reimbursement
- **Professional Memberships**: Industry association dues covered

## Global Payroll Considerations

### Geographic Adjustments

- **Cost of Living**: Base salaries adjusted by Mercer cost of living index
- **Local Market Rates**: Benchmarked against local tech sector compensation
- **Currency**: Salaries paid in local currency with exchange rate protection

### International Subsidiaries

| Region | Base Adjustment | Benefits Compliance | Tax Optimization |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| North America | +0-5% | ACA, HIPAA, ERISA | 401(k) tax advantages |
| Europe | +10-15% | GDPR, Working Time Directive | Pension tax relief |
| Asia-Pacific | -5% to +5% | Local labor laws | Tax-efficient structures |
| Australia | +15-20% | Fair Work Act | Superannuation requirements |
| Canada | +5-10% | Provincial labor codes | RRSP tax benefits |

### Compliance Requirements

- **Minimum Wage**: Compliance with all local minimum wage laws
- **Overtime**: 1.5x regular rate for hours over 40/week
- **Taxes**: Withholding and remittance of all applicable taxes
- **Immigration**: Work permit and visa compliance for international hires

## Payroll Processing Procedures

### Pay Cycles

- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly (26 pay periods annually)
- **Pay Dates**: Every other Friday
- **Direct Deposit**: Mandatory for all employees
- **Payroll Calendar**: Published quarterly with holiday adjustments

### Deductions & Withholdings

- **Federal/State Taxes**: Automatic calculation and withholding
- **Social Security/Medicare**: Employee and employer contributions
- **Health Insurance**: Pre-tax deductions for medical premiums
- **Retirement Contributions**: Pre-tax 401(k) deferrals
- **Garnishments**: Court-ordered deductions processed immediately

### Year-End Processing

- **W-2 Forms**: Distributed by January 31
- **1099 Forms**: For contractors and consultants
- **Tax Reporting**: Compliance with all federal and state requirements
- **Bonus Payments**: Processed separately from regular payroll

## Performance Review & Compensation Adjustments

### Annual Review Process

- **Self-Assessment**: Employee self-evaluation
- **Manager Review**: Performance feedback and rating
- **Calibration Meetings**: Cross-functional performance alignment
- **Compensation Decisions**: Merit increases and bonus allocations

### Merit Increases

- **Timing**: Effective April 1 following annual review
- **Budget**: 3-5% annual increase pool based on company performance
- **Individual Increases**: 0-8% based on performance rating and market positioning

### Promotion Guidelines

- **Salary Adjustment**: 10-20% increase for level changes
- **Equity Grants**: Additional equity for significant promotions
- **Bonus Target**: Increased percentage for higher-level roles

## Termination & Final Pay

### Voluntary Termination

- **Notice Period**: 2 weeks for non-exempt, 4 weeks for exempt
- **Final Paycheck**: Includes accrued vacation and unused PTO
- **Benefits Continuation**: COBRA coverage available
- **Exit Interview**: Required for all departing employees

### Involuntary Termination

- **Severance Package**: 2 weeks base pay per year of service (minimum 8 weeks)
- **Outplacement Services**: Career counseling and job search support
- **Equity Treatment**: Accelerated vesting of 50% unvested equity
- **Reference Policy**: Neutral references provided

## Policy Administration

### Oversight

- **HR Committee**: Reviews and approves compensation changes
- **Compensation Committee**: Sets executive compensation
- **Board Approval**: Required for significant policy changes

### Communication

- **Employee Handbook**: Annual distribution and acknowledgment
- **Payroll Portal**: Self-service access to pay stubs and W-2s
- **Training**: Annual compliance training for managers

### Audits & Compliance

- **Internal Audits**: Quarterly payroll process reviews
- **External Audits**: Annual third-party payroll audit
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Continuous monitoring of labor law changes
